Introduction

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at Peking University, working with Prof. Lu Zhang. I received my Ph.D. from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT) in 2023, and was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Hui Liu and Prof. Lu Zhang. During 2019-2020, I spent a wonderful year as a visiting student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, worked with Prof. Darko Marinov.

Her research interests include software refactoring and software testing.
